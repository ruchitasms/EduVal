Analysis Framework

Enterprise Multiple (EV/EBITDA)

This ratio measures how many years of cash profit (EBITDA) it would take to pay back the cost of acquiring the entire business. It is a sector-agnostic way to find value across different industries.

Leverage Context (Net Debt/EBITDA)

This is a fundamental "Health Check." It represents a company's ability to handle its debt load. In high-growth tech (NVDA), this is often near zero; in capital-heavy sectors (XOM), stability is key.
