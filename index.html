Financial Analysis Framework

Enterprise Multiple (EV/EBITDA)

This ratio measures how many years of cash profit it would take to buy the entire business (Equity + Debt - Cash). It is more comprehensive than P/E because it ignores differences in how companies are taxed or financed.

Leverage Context (Net Debt/EBITDA)

This is a solvency check. It tells us how many years of current earnings would be required to pay off all debt. In the Energy sector, a ratio below 2.0x is generally considered very strong.
