Relative Valuation

Instead of guessing a stock's "true" value in a vacuum, we compare it to peers. If XOM has an EV/EBITDA of 8x while the sector average is 10x, it is "relatively" cheap.

EV/EBITDA (The Cash Multiple)

Calculated as EV / EBITDA. It tells you how much you're paying for every dollar of operational cash flow. In energy, lower is generally better value.
