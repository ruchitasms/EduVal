EduVal is an interactive learning platform designed to translate complex financial data into intuitive insights. Most retail investors look at price; data professionals look at the "engine" behind the price. This project is a work-in-progress journey to bridge the gap between Financial Literacy and Data Science, helping users see the "truth" behind the tickers.

### üìä Core Framework (Current Features)
Phase 1: The Valuation Verdict Engine
Contextual Multiples: Automatically categorizes stocks into Value Plays, Fair Value, or Premium Growth based on their live EV/EBITDA multiples.
Sector-Specific Intelligence: The UI dynamically adjusts its theme and logic (e.g., Energy vs. Software) to reflect different market expectations.
Momentum Tracking: Real-time 7-day price visualization using Chart.js to compare market sentiment against fundamental value.

Phase 2: Financial Statement Deep Dive (New!)
The "Report Card" View: A dedicated dashboard for the Balance Sheet, Income Statement, and Cash Flow.
Automated Logic Badges: * Liquidity (Healthy vs. Risk): Checks if Current Assets can cover immediate liabilities.
Efficiency (High Moat vs. Standard): Identifies companies with pricing power through high Net Margins.
Quality (High Quality vs. Accrual Warning): Validates if reported profits are backed by actual Free Cash Flow.
Bridge Metrics: Integrated EBITDA and Enterprise Value directly into the statement views to show the link between a company‚Äôs operations and its market price.
